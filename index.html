About the Restaurant

This restaurant was started on the motive of serving the public with mouth watering delicious food items.Hotel Muruga Bhavan Multicuisine restaurant group was started in 1999. Now with more than 10 branches in South India and UAE we have had a steady growth and established ourselves as a leading Restaurant chain offering exquisite Arabian Savouries. Our chain of restaurants are now in Bangalore, Chennai, Mysore and UAE. We plan to add 6 more branches in the coming year. Our objective is to be known as the most favored customer friendly restaurant chain offering authentic Arabian Cuisine and Multi Cuisine in all the major cities in South India.
